Fun Facts

Established Since 2014

L'eclat Consult is a firm of Chartered Accountants, Tax and Management Consultants registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC). The firm offers a wide range of financial/accounting and tax services to clients in various sectors of the economy. Our practice is adequately staffed with young, dynamic, competent and well-qualified Nigerians who have acquired quality professional training and first-class experiences from within and outside the country.

Our staff are exposed to business information in the global market with special understanding of the Nigerian business environment. We have been providing high quality Statutory Audit, Tax and Management services to organizations of various sizes in the private and public sector of the economy.
